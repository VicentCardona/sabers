Frequently Asked Questions

▸ What software does my student need?
All software used in this class is free. We primarily use Python, Visual Studio Code, and web-based tools like Tinkercad.
▸ How are grades calculated?
Grades are based 40% on projects, 40% on exams, and 20% on weekly participation and homework assignments.
▸ How can I contact the teacher?
The best way to reach me is via the school email system. I typically respond within 24 business hours.
